Red cross

In my third year at computer science we had a big project with a customer. We worked for Red Cross Trondheim. Our task was to improve and extend their shift management system. We were 6 people working on it for half a year and it is written in php. The system had many inconsistencies in the design and we wanted to improve the user experience.

The pictures with title Version 1 is the old system that we wanted to improve and Version 2 is our end result. We focused on giving the pages more meaningful content and easy for the red cross team to get a quick overview over their shifts.
