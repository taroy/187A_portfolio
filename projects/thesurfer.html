The surfer

This was a project I had in the fall 2011, in CSE Server-sided Web Applications. We developed a surf site where you could check your local surf news and weather conditions at beaches in California

The project didn't focus on the design, but we still wanted to make it userfriendly and look as good as possible.

This project is written in HTML, Jsp and Java
